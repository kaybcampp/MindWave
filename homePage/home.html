About MindWave

MindWave is your personal wellness companion, designed to support mental health with an interactive experience.

Features:

  • Chat: Communicate directly with your therapist or access helpful tips from our AI assistant.
  • Milestone Tracking: Set and track daily goals that encourage a healthier, happier routine.
  • Notes: Reflect, journal, and gain insights to better understand your journey. Share reflections as you choose.
